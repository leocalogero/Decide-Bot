Hangry?

Not familiar with the term? Hangriness is real, infact it was recently added to the Online Oxford Dictionary and its use is growing in popularity. Just as it sounds, "hangry" derived from two human states:

hun·gry

/ˈhəNGɡrē/

adjective
synonyms: ravenous, empty, in need of food, hollow, faint from/with hunger;

feeling or displaying the need for food.

an·gry

/ˈaNGɡrē/

adjective
angry; comparative adjective: angrier; superlative adjective: angriest

having a strong feeling of or showing annoyance, displeasure, or hostility; full of anger.

han·gry

/ˈhaNGɡrē/

adjective informal
adjective: hangry; comparative adjective: hangrier; superlative adjective: hangriest

An amalgum of hungry and angry invented to describe when you are so hungry that your lack of food causes you to become angry, frustrated or both.
